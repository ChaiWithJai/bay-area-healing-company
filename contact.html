FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I hear back? +

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please mention it in your message.

What happens on the discovery call? +

It's a casual 20-minute conversation where we learn about your goals, answer your questions, and help you determine which program would be the best fit. No pressure, no pitch.

Do you work with companies? +

Yes! We offer corporate workshops, team retreats, and custom programs for organizations. Select "Corporate/Team Programs" in the form above or mention it in your message.

I'm not in the Bay Area. Can we still work together? +

Absolutely. Our online cohorts and self-paced courses are available worldwide. In-person workshops and retreats are in Northern California, but we welcome participants from anywhere.
