How It Works

FAQ

Common Questions

How is this different from meditation apps or YouTube videos? +

Apps and videos give you techniques. We give you structure tied to your specific goals. You'll know exactly what to do, when, and why it matters for the outcome you're working toward. Plus, you get feedback and community ‚Äî not just content consumption.

I'm skeptical about "wellness." Is this going to be woo-woo? +

No crystals, no chakras, no vague promises. Everything we teach is grounded in evidence ‚Äî behavioral science, nervous system regulation, and measurable outcomes. If you can't track it, we don't teach it.

How much time do I need to commit? +

Depends on the program. Self-paced courses: work at your own speed. Cohorts: about 3-4 hours/week including practice. Workshops: one-time commitment. Retreats: 2-7 days all-in. We design for busy people ‚Äî the practices themselves are 10-20 minutes daily.

What if I start and realize it's not for me? +

All programs come with a satisfaction guarantee. For cohorts and courses, you can request a full refund within the first two weeks if you've engaged with the material and it's not working for you. For retreats, we offer rescheduling or credit toward future programs.

Do you work with companies/teams? +

Yes. We run corporate workshops and team programs for companies that want to invest in their people's performance and well-being. Custom programs available. Reach out via the contact form to discuss.

I'm not in the Bay Area. Can I still participate? +

Absolutely. All our cohorts and courses are available online with participants from around the world. Only in-person workshops require Bay Area attendance. Retreats are in Northern California but open to anyone who can travel.
