The Experience

How Cohorts Work

1

Apply & Get Matched

Submit a brief application. We'll schedule a 15-minute call to ensure the cohort is the right fit for your goals.

2

Onboarding Week

Before the first session, you'll receive prep materials, meet your cohort mates, and set your specific goals.

3

Weekly Sessions

Join live 90-minute sessions each week. Expect teaching, guided practice, breakout discussions, and Q&A.

4

Daily Practice

Complete 10-20 minute daily practices between sessions. Track your progress and share wins in Slack.

5

1:1 Check-ins

Two private 30-minute sessions with Aaron (mid-program and final week) to customize your approach.

6

Integration & Alumni

Graduate with your personalized practice plan. Join the alumni community for ongoing support.
